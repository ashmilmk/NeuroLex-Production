Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit 3 students on the Free plan?
You won't lose access to existing students — you simply can't add new ones until you upgrade. Your 3 students remain fully active.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes! Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
The Free plan is your trial — use all core features with up to 3 students at no cost, for as long as you want.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via our secure Stripe-powered checkout.
