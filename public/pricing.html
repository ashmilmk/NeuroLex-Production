Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when I hit 3 students on the Free plan?
You won't lose access to your existing students — you simply can't add new ones until you upgrade. Your 3 existing students remain fully active.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
The Free plan itself is your trial — you can use all core features with up to 3 students at no cost, for as long as you want.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) via our secure Stripe-powered checkout. No card data touches our servers.
